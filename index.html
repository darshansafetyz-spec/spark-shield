Material Science

WHY VERMICULITE?

The mineral that makes Spark Shield the gold standard in industrial heat protection.

WHAT IS VERMICULITE?
Vermiculite is a naturally occurring hydrated magnesium iron aluminium silicate mineral. When applied as a coating over woven fibreglass, it creates an incredibly robust protective layer. The mineral's unique crystalline structure allows it to withstand direct exposure to flames, weld spatter, and radiant heat that would destroy ordinary protective materials. SPARK SHIELD blankets are coated using a proprietary suspension process that ensures even, durable coverage on every blanket produced.
HEAT EXPANSION
Under intense heat, vermiculite undergoes exfoliation — expanding up to 30× its original volume. This creates millions of microscopic air pockets that act as a powerful thermal barrier, dramatically reducing heat transfer through the blanket to any protected surface or equipment beneath.
INDUSTRIAL BENEFITS
Non-combustible. Chemically inert. Lightweight. Reusable. Vermiculite-coated blankets provide consistent protection against weld spatter, hot slag, and radiant heat — making them the preferred choice in shipyards, steel plants, refineries, and defence across India and internationally.
